About Home Revival Brisbane

Home Revival Brisbane exists to take the stress out of finding help when something goes wrong around the house. We’re a small Brisbane-based team with one mission: make it simple to get the job done right — by someone you can trust.

Whether it’s a leaking tap, power issue, broken fence, urgent yard cleanup or something more unique, we connect you to a reliable local professional who’s ready to help — no searching, no uncertainty, no wasted weekends.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Real People: We’re not an app or a marketplace. We’re locals who know the area — and know who’s worth calling.
  • Hand-Picked Help: We only work with professionals we’d feel confident sending to our own homes.
  • No Guesswork: You tell us what you need. We match it to the right person. It’s that easy.
  • Fast Turnaround: Most requests are connected within the same day, often within hours.
  • We Work With All Jobs: Big or small, urgent or flexible — if you need help, we’ll find the right person for it.

Why We Do It

We started Home Revival Brisbane after seeing how hard it was for everyday people to find honest, available trades or service providers — especially when time mattered. Our team wanted to bring back the human touch to an increasingly impersonal space.

With us, you’re never just a number in a queue. You’re a neighbour — and we’ll treat your situation with care and urgency like it’s our own.

Locally Run. Personally Involved.

We’re Brisbane locals who live and work in the same communities we serve. That means we care about quality, word-of-mouth, and every bit of feedback you give. We’re here for the long term, not just a transaction.
