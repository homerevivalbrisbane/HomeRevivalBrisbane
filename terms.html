We do not provide the actual services ourselves and are not responsible for the quality, timeliness, or any other aspect of the services delivered by third-party professionals.

2. Service Provider Selection

We carefully select professionals based on our experience and knowledge of local businesses to provide you with the most appropriate help. However, we cannot guarantee the outcome or satisfaction from any particular service provider.

3. Service Fee

A one-time coordination fee is charged to you for using our service. This fee varies depending on the type of service requested:

  • Electrical and Plumbing: $50 coordination fee
  • General Household Services (Handyman, Painting, Carpentry): $30 coordination fee
  • Cleaning, Gardening, Lawn Mowing: $20 coordination fee
  • Other: Initial $20 coordination fee. Additional fees may apply depending on the professionals required (max $50).

This fee is payable upon form submission and is used solely to coordinate your request. If no suitable provider is found, the fee will be refunded in full.

4. Payment and Pricing

Pricing for services is determined solely by the service providers. Home Revival Brisbane does not handle or control payments for services rendered.

  • If no replacement can be found, a refund may be offered at our discretion.
  • Choosing to cancel after a replacement is offered does not entitle a refund.
